Data Deletion Instructions

To delete your FoodOrderApp account and data, choose one of the options below.

Option A: In-App “Delete my account” (recommended)

  1. Open the app and go to Profile → Settings → Delete my account.
  2. Confirm. This will permanently delete your account, authentication record, and associated data stored in Firebase.

Option B: Request by Email

Email us at from the email address linked to your account and include the subject “Delete my FoodOrderApp data”. We will delete your account and associated data within 30 days and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Firebase Authentication user record (email, UID, provider info).
  • User data stored in our database/storage linked to your UID (e.g., profile, orders, preferences).
